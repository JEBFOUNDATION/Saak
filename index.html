The JEB Foundation

Our Projects

JEB Foundation is an initiative for the less privilege; it is a charitable non-profit organization.

It is solely dedicated to improving the welfare of children in Nigeria.


JEB Foundation seeks to mobilize the efforts of the government, the private sector and other development agencies towards securing the future of the less privilege children via educational opportunities for children in disadvantaged families and deprived communities.

JEB Foundation’s work is aimed at enhancing social development by improving the lives of vulnerable and underprivileged children in our society.


Our work comprises continuous and time-bound initiatives as well as research studies that focus on the various priority areas.


JEB Foundation’s programs are continuous initiatives designed to help the organization achieve its mission of reaching out to children in need.

Make A Donation

JEB Foundation relies on regular financial and in-kind support of individuals and organizations to fund our rehabilitation, education and empowerment programmes.




Your frequent support and assistance in whatever capacity made to our programmes will ensure sustainability and development of our work, individuals we cater for and to deliver social reformation to achieve individual and community transformation.

Our Objectives


To provide support to Orphans and Vulnerable Children in Lagos and other states in Nigeria.


Our Mission


To reach out, give hope, rehabilitate, educate and empower impoverished children.


Our Vision


To help the less priveleged children in Nigeria.
